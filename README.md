# TRS Crew Portal

> **The Royal Sorcerers (TRS)** – A premium GTA Online (PC) automotive community website built for organizing, managing, and showcasing car meets, bike meets, events, and crew activities.

🌐 **Live Website:** https://trs-crew.vercel.app

---

## About

TRS Crew Portal is the official website for **The Royal Sorcerers**, a GTA Online PC automotive community.

The platform provides members with everything they need in one place:

* Upcoming Meet Information
* Vehicle Registry
* Previous Meets Archive
* Crew Gallery
* Meet Rules & Regulations
* Crew Portal
* Admin Dashboard
* Member Management
* Featured Builds
* Community Feedback
* Live Countdown Timer
* Meet Documents & Downloads

Designed with a cinematic and premium aesthetic inspired by modern automotive brands and AAA game interfaces.

## Vehicle Registry

The Vehicle Registry allows organizers to publish complete lists of vehicles for upcoming meets.

Each registry supports:

* Registry Topic
* Description
* Vehicle List
* Image
* Approved / Restricted categories
* Search
* Filters
* Vehicle Counter
* Expandable Registry Cards
* PDF Download
* Admin Management
